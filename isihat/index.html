The algorithm

Biologically weighted, not streak-dependent

Most habit apps treat every action as equal. NeuroHabit doesn't. The Foundational Gate Formula multiplies each category score by a biological readiness factor — your sleep, hydration, and nutrition completion.

If your physiological foundations are incomplete, all higher-order habit scores are penalised. This mirrors how neurochemical systems actually work: Breuning (2012), Harvey et al. (2022).

Users never see the number. They see a Brain Phase — a qualitative descriptor that communicates readiness without exposing a gameable score.

Effective Score
  = rawPct
  × (0.5 + 0.5 × foundationRate)
rawPct
Proportion of habits completed within a neurochemical category on a given day [0.0–1.0]
foundationRate
Proportion of W3 foundational habits completed that day [0.0–1.0]
Multiplier range
0.5 (zero foundation) → 1.0 (full foundation). All higher-order habits scaled by biological readiness.
