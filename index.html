About

I believe that access to clear, accurate health information is a right — not a privilege. Too often, people make healthcare decisions based on incomplete or misleading information.

Aufthority is my attempt to change that. I research, write, and build — creating content and tools grounded in science, designed for real people navigating their own health journeys.
